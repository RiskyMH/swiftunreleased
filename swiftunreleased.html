About

We are a team who are based in the USA and Australia. We have been collecting Unreleased songs for years and have gained a nice collection over the time we have been collecting. Swift Unreleased™ was established in June of 2020. This platform is aimed for trading with other unreleased collectors, to try and gain more songs. We DO NOT leak songs, or give any out for free without another trade in return. There might be some rarer songs that will require a rarer trade, but other than that, we try and make the trade as fair as possible. You must send us your trade first, and then we will follow up with the song(s) you require in return. We also must chat first before ANY trading takes place. If you do not send the song that we requested, we will not follow up with the trade. If you request for a song you give us NOT to go onto our trading list (and into potential circulation), we will fufill your request. If you have any worries or conerns about our trading policy, please feel free to Contact Us
